General Mess Rules

All hostel students are required to follow the dining guidelines set by the VIT Mess Administration. Violations may result in suspension of mess privileges.

View All Regulations
R.01
ID card is mandatory for entry to any mess facility. Entry without a valid card will be denied.
R.02
Mess switch requests must be submitted through the official portal before the 25th of every month.
R.03
Food wastage is strictly prohibited. Students must take only what they intend to consume.
R.04
Outside food items are not permitted inside the mess hall. Meal times are strictly observed.
R.05
Dress code must be maintained. Sleeveless attire and slippers without socks are not permitted.
R.06
Any complaint or feedback must be submitted through the official feedback system, not verbally to staff.
