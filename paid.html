Hotel-Style Dining

Paid Mess

Restaurant-quality meals, flexible ordering, no fixed schedule. Available at Central Mess B and Central Mess D.

What is the Paid Mess?

Unlike subscription-based messes, the Paid Mess operates like a campus restaurant. Students order meals at the counter and pay per item.

01
No Monthly Subscription

Pay only for what you eat. No advance payment or fixed monthly fee required. Suitable for students with irregular schedules.

02
Extended Hours

Open from 7:00 AM to 10:30 PM daily, including weekends and holidays. Late-night snacks and beverages available after 9 PM.

03
A La Carte Ordering

Choose individual items or pre-set combo meals. Display menu is updated weekly. Seasonal specials added on weekends.

04
Meal Combos Available

Curated meal combos starting at Rs. 80. Includes main course, side, and beverage. Cheaper than ordering individually.

05
Both Veg and Non-Veg

Full range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian items available every day. Jain and no-onion options on request.

06
Digital Payment Accepted

Pay via UPI, VIT card wallet, or cash. Wallet top-up can be done at the mess office or through the student portal.

Paid Mess Rules and Regulations

Students using the Paid Mess facility are required to follow these guidelines without exception.

Ordering and Payment
P.01All orders must be placed at the counter. No table service is available. Students must collect their tray themselves.
P.02Payment must be made at the time of ordering. No credit or deferred payment is permitted.
P.03Once an order is placed and paid, it cannot be cancelled. Exchanges may be made within 2 minutes of collection if there is a preparation error.
P.04VIT wallet top-ups can only be done at the mess office during office hours (9 AM to 5 PM, Mon to Sat).
P.05Receipts are issued for every transaction above Rs. 50. Students should retain receipts for dispute resolution.
Conduct and Entry
P.06VIT student ID card must be presented at entry. External guests are not permitted inside the Paid Mess facility.
P.07The last order will be taken 30 minutes before closing time. No new orders accepted after 10:00 PM.
P.08Tables must be vacated within 30 minutes of receiving food during peak hours (12:30 PM to 2:00 PM and 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM).
P.09Mobile phones should be on silent mode inside the dining area. Loud calls are not permitted at the tables.
P.10Any hygiene or quality complaints must be submitted through the official feedback form, not to mess staff directly.

VIT Mess Wallet

The VIT Mess Wallet is a prepaid digital wallet linked to your student ID. Load it once and pay seamlessly at the Paid Mess counter without carrying cash.

    W.01Minimum top-up amount: Rs. 200. Maximum wallet balance allowed: Rs. 5,000.
    W.02Wallet balance can be checked at the mess office or at the payment kiosk near the counter.
    W.03Unused balance at semester end can be transferred to the next semester or refunded upon request.
    W.04Wallet is non-transferable. It can only be used by the registered student.
