Mess Rules and Regulations

All hostel students registered for any mess type must adhere to the following regulations.

General Conduct
G.01ID card is mandatory for entry. Students without a valid ID will not be permitted inside the mess hall.
G.02Students must maintain orderly queues at the serving counter. Cutting queues or reserving seats for others is not permitted.
G.03Food wastage is strictly prohibited. Students must take only the quantity they will consume. Repeat servings are available for rice and dal.
G.04Food items must not be taken out of the mess hall in personal containers or bags.
G.05Dress code must be observed. Students in nightwear, sleeveless tops without covering, or barefoot are not permitted entry.
Fee and Registration
F.01Mess fee must be paid in full before the 10th of each month. A late fee of Rs. 50 per day applies after the due date.
F.02Mess fee is non-refundable once the month has commenced, except for approved medical or emergency leave applications.
F.03Students may switch mess type only during the switch window (20th to 25th of each month). Retroactive switches are not permitted.
F.04Non-payment of mess fee for more than two consecutive months will result in suspension of access and academic hold.
F.05Mess fee refunds for approved leaves are processed within 10 working days to the student's bank account on record.
Hygiene and Safety
H.01Students must wash hands at the wash basins provided before collecting their tray. Hand sanitizer dispensers are available at entry.
H.02Used trays and utensils must be deposited at the designated collection counters. Students must not leave trays on tables.
H.03Any spillage must be reported to the mess staff immediately. Students must not attempt to clean up on their own in the kitchen area.
H.04Students with communicable illness should not dine in the mess hall. Meals can be collected in approved containers for in-room consumption with warden approval.
Complaints and Feedback
C.01All complaints must be submitted through the official online feedback or grievance form. Verbal complaints to kitchen staff will not be officially registered.
C.02Grievances are reviewed by the Mess Committee every Monday. Students will receive a written response within 3 working days.
C.03Anonymous feedback is accepted and reviewed. However, for billing or refund-related complaints, student identification is mandatory.
C.04Misuse of the grievance system by submitting false or malicious complaints will result in disciplinary action.
