Nutritional Standards

All VIT mess kitchens follow FSSAI guidelines. Menus are designed by a certified nutritionist to ensure balanced macros and micronutrients for hostel students. Seasonal vegetables are prioritised.

  • Minimum 2500 kcal per day per student
  • Protein-rich dal, legumes, and eggs included daily
  • No artificial colour or flavour additives
  • RO-purified water used in all cooking
  • Hygiene audit conducted every 15 days
